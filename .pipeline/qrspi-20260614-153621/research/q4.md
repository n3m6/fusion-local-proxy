## Findings for Q4

### Summary
Both SDKs expose overloaded `create()` methods discriminated by `stream` boolean literal, dedicated `stream()` convenience methods returning rich stream objects, structured-output parameters at the request-body level (`response_format` for OpenAI, `output_config.format` for Anthropic), and `AbortSignal` passthrough via a shared `RequestOptions.signal` field plus first-class `AbortController` on stream objects. The types were extracted from the actual installed packages: `openai@6.42.0` and `@anthropic-ai/sdk@0.104.1`.
